The Resort

A quiet harbor, minutes from Alona Beach

Tucked along Ester A. Lim Drive in Tawala, Alona Mariners Resort keeps a sailor's calm at the heart of Panglao.

  • Courtyard swimming pool with cascade waterwall and sun deck
  • Spacious air-conditioned rooms with balcony or terrace
  • Complimentary breakfast, poolside snack bar and billiards lounge
  • ~20-minute stroll to Alona Beach · 5-minute drive to the airport
