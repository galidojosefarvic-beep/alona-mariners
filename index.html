The Resort

A quiet harbor, minutes from Alona Beach

Tucked along Ester A. Lim Drive in Tawala, Alona Mariners Resort keeps a sailor's calm at the heart of Panglao. Handcrafted wooden balconies look down on a mosaic-tiled pool and its gentle cascade wall; the "Welcome Aboard" lounge pours cold drinks beneath a ship's wheel; and every morning begins with breakfast on the house.

  • Courtyard swimming pool with cascade waterwall and sun deck
  • Spacious air-conditioned rooms with balcony or terrace, cable TV, fridge and kettle
  • Complimentary breakfast, poolside snack bar and billiards lounge
  • ~20-minute stroll to Alona Beach · 5-minute drive to Bohol–Panglao International Airport

Beyond the Resort

Voyages from your doorstep

Panglao and the Bohol mainland hold the Philippines' great natural treasures — all within an easy day of your room.

~1.5 km · 20-min stroll

Alona Beach

Panglao's famous strip of powder sand — swim, dine barefoot, and watch the paraw sails at sunset.
~6 km · 12-min drive

Panglao White Beaches

Dumaluan and White Beach — long, calm and crowd-free, with knee-deep turquoise shallows.
Boat from Alona · ~45 min

Balicasag Island Hopping

Dolphin watching at dawn, then snorkel Balicasag's marine sanctuary with sea turtles and jackfish.
Dauis · ~15-min drive

Hinagdanan Cave

A limestone cavern of stalactites with an emerald swimmable lagoon, lit by natural skylights.
Carmen · ~2-hr tour

Chocolate Hills

Over a thousand symmetrical mounds turning cocoa-brown in summer — Bohol's iconic horizon.
Corella · ~45-min drive

Tarsier Sanctuary

Meet one of the world's smallest primates at the Philippine Tarsier Sanctuary — quiet voices required.

Our front desk arranges island hopping, countryside tours, transfers and motorbike hire — simply ask at check-in.
